# Bundle 4 — DeFi Flow Intelligence

## Status
Planning Phase

---

# Purpose

DeFi Flow Intelligence provides machine-readable temporal awareness of capital movement across DeFi ecosystems.

The bundle focuses on:
- liquidity migration
- bridge movement
- stablecoin rotation
- protocol inflow/outflow pressure
- cross-chain capital behavior
- ecosystem acceleration/deceleration

The objective is not raw feed aggregation alone.

The objective is structured temporal context for autonomous systems.
